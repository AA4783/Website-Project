Competition

Meet preparation

Getting prepared for a meet is crucial to performing well and doing the best you can, here are some tips to help:

  • Create a training plan that will allow you to get into the best shape you can in the timeframe you have, every practice is crucial.
  • Make sure to clean up your diet and sleep well as this will allow you to get the most out of runs and keep you healthy for race day.
  • Focus on your training and doing everything you can to arrive on rcae day confident in yourself and your ability.
  • Practice is key, however it is important not to overtrain or overexert yourself as that could lead to injury and be detrimental to your performance.

Race day

This is it, the big day, your time to shine. It can be absolutely terrifying, but these small things can help keep your mind at ease and yourself positive:

  • Remember your training, the hardest workouts, and all your preparation. This will keep you grounded and give you the confidence you need.
  • Visualize whatever it is you want to achieve in that race or over the course of the meet, imagine every single deatil and what you want to happen. Hold onto that image in your head and know that you can make it a reality.
  • Sometimes you may be scared out of your mind. Listening to your favorite music, or something that will get you into the right headspace helps keep you calm and relaxed in order to prepare for the event.
  • Believe in yourself, have faith in your training, and clear all negative thoughts from your head.
  • Take some time to slow your breathing and relax to conserve your energy.

Your moment to shine

Don't doubt yourself, you can do this. Race day is the culmination of all your hard work to this point, trust in yourself and your abilities. Whatever happens just know you will be a better runner for it, if you get that win take some time to celebrate and get ready for the next meet, ride that high. If you don't ahcieve your goal that's okay, you now know what you need to do next time to crush the competition the next time, don't get down on yourself, take some time to reflect and remember there will always be another oppurtunity.
