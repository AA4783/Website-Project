Training

Training in General

If you want to improve and become a better runner, you need to train just like in every other sport or anything you want to get better at. The best way to do so is to practice. Each person will train differently depending on their purpose. Those who actively compete will train much more than those who run recreationally, this differs for each person.

Training tips

  • Train at the load appropriate for you, don't overdo it meaning start with a lower training volume and build up.
  • Make sure to hydrate properly in order to help prepare your body for the workout and recover.
  • Create a training plan and stick to it, this is the best way to ensure that your going to meet your goals.
  • Ensure that you get enough sleep, as you won't perform at your best unless you have plenty of rest.
