Why Run?

Health Benefits

There are numerous health benefits to running some of them include:

  • Improved mental health due to the release of endorphins which make you happier, want to run longer, and help you clear your head by releasing stress.
  • Improved cardiovascular health and endurnace, running helps keep your heart strong and healthy.
  • Overall improvements to physical health, running exercises your whole body which helps keep you healthy and productive.

Other benefits

Some other non health related benefits include:

  • Running has the potential to provde you with a new community and friends.
  • Improvements in mood and productivity can be attributed to running.
  • Higher pain threshold and increased mental toughness, which help you tackle challenging situations with more ease.

How to get into running

Running isn't something that everyone enjoys and you'll never know if you like it or not until you try it. Sure it’s more difficult than other sports and can seem daunting. However, if you do end up enjoying it, it’s something you can do anytime anywhere. If you want to get into running the only way to do it is to get out there and run. Time, distance, and duration don’t matter, just start and you’ll naturally figure out when it’s time to stop.
